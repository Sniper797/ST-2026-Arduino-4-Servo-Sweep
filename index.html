What "Sweep" is

Sweep is the stock example that ships with Servo.h. It walks the horn one degree at a time from 0° to 180°, waits 15 ms between each degree, then walks all the way back. Repeat forever. That slow, continuous windscreen-wiper motion is the sweep.

Why it stops at 133°

One full 0→180 pass costs 180 × 15 ms ≈ 2.7 s — longer than the 2 s the task allows. So the sweep is cut off mid-travel at 2000 / 15 ≈ 133 steps. The dashed ghost trace shows where it would have kept going.

Then: hold

At the 2 s mark writeAll(90) runs once and loop() is left empty. Nothing else ever commands the servos, so they sit at 90° — powered, resisting load — until reset or power-off.
